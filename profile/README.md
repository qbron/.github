## `hej!` 👋

In Swedish, **bron** means *bridge*. So **Qbron** is the **Q**uantum **bron**.
We are *aggressively* into wordplay. You've been warned.

We're building the open-source SDK + Nordic-resident managed cloud that lets you write a quantum circuit **once** and route it to whichever QPU is cheapest, fastest, or least likely to be on fire today — IBM, IQM, AWS Braket, Azure, Pasqal, your laptop, the toaster (eventually).

Think *Vercel for quantum*. Or *LiteLLM for QPUs*. Or the bridge from Asgard to Midgard, but for qubits. 🌈⚡

---

## ⚛️ the three pillars

**01 · One API, every backend.** Write your circuit once. Route it to the cheapest, fastest, or most accurate QPU automatically.

**02 · Open-source by default.** Apache-2.0. Self-host it. Audit it. Fork it. Carve your own runes.

**03 · Sovereign by design.** EU-resident data plane. GDPR-native. Built for European research and regulated enterprise from commit `0`.
